Frequently Asked Questions

What is the CompliTrst Partner Agency Program? +
It's a turnkey license to operate your own compliance agency using the CompliTrst™ platform. You receive training, certification, editable SOPs and templates, software seats, marketing tools, and ongoing support so you can deliver high-value compliance services to assisted living facilities and group homes.
Who qualifies to join? +
We welcome experienced nurses, healthcare professionals, consultants, and compliance professionals who want to build a profitable agency. If you have a passion for helping facilities stay compliant and are ready to invest in your own business, you're a great fit.
What's included in the program? +
Depending on your tier (Starter, Pro, or Elite), you get full certification, business setup, customizable policies and forms, a pre-built website, 5–15 facility license seats, group coaching, and access to our partner community. Elite partners also receive city/zip exclusivity, recruiting rights, and a 1-on-1 coaching call with Dr. TMac.

Starter partners pay $7,500 upfront with $297/month after a 60-day grace period. Pro partners pay $12,500 upfront with $447/month, and Elite partners pay $20,000 upfront with $697/month. How do I make money as a partner? +
You charge facilities a setup fee (typically $3,500–$5,000) and a monthly retainer (often $1,000 per facility) for compliance management. Partners who manage 6–10 facilities can generate six-figure annual income.
What is the "Go Live in 30" bonus? +
If you onboard your first facility within 30 days of enrolling, you'll unlock an extra facility license seat at no extra cost — the 6th seat for Starter, 11th for Pro, or 16th for Elite.
Do I need to be in Florida? +
Although the program is piloted in Florida, we welcome consultants from anywhere in the United States. You can serve clients remotely and expand to other states as we grow.
What happens during the first 60 days? +
Your first two months are fee-free, giving you time to complete your certification, customize your business setup, and onboard your first facility without any monthly charges.
Is there support after I sign up? +
Yes. You'll receive ongoing group coaching, access to a private partner community, and help desk support. Elite partners also receive a dedicated coaching call with Dr. TMac.
