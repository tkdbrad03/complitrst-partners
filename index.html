Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need to train or onboard the facility after I refer them? +
No. Your only role is to refer the facility. We handle all demos, onboarding, and support directly with the client.
How much do I earn for each referral? +
You earn a $300 referral bonus for every facility that signs up through your link, plus 20% monthly recurring commission for as long as they remain an active subscriber.
Is there a limit to how many facilities I can refer? +
No. There's no cap. You can refer as many facilities as you want.
Who can I refer? +
You can refer any licensed or soon-to-be-licensed facility in Florida that operates under APD, DCF, or AHCA — including group homes, ALFs, and residential programs.
How do I track my referrals and payouts? +

Once you're approved, you'll receive a unique referral link.

We use this link to privately track all activity connected to your referrals. You'll receive a monthly report showing:

  • How many people clicked your link
  • How many completed the application
  • How many became paying clients

Referral activity is tracked for 90 days from the first click, giving you credit even if someone signs up later. No login is required — your report comes directly to your email.

How do I get paid? +

Referral bonuses and monthly commissions are processed after the referred facility completes onboarding and payment.

Here's how it works:

  • Referral Reports Sent: 1st of each month
  • Payouts Processed: By the 5th of each month

You'll be paid via the method you selected during onboarding, and you'll receive a detailed breakdown of each referral and payout amount.

Do I need to live in Florida to become a partner? +
No. You just need to know or be connected to facility owners in Florida.
Is this an affiliate program? +
No. This is a direct partner referral opportunity — not a public affiliate program. All partners are approved individually.
